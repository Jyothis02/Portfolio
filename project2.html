Air Travel Management

This project is a simplified flight booking website where users can select their departure and destination cities, view available flights, and proceed to booking. The interface is user-friendly, with input boxes for selecting routes and a clean display of flight options. After choosing a flight, users are prompted to log in to confirm their booking. The login system ensures basic authentication before proceeding.
